# 90-Day Pilot Go-Live Playbook (2 Cities)

## Objective
Launch and validate AsanAvto trust-led marketplace model in two cities with measurable growth and risk control.

## Pilot Scope
- City 1: Baku (primary liquidity market)
- City 2: Ganja (secondary validation market)
- Segments: Hybrid (C2C + B2B)

## Pilot Targets (Day 90)
- 30 active dealerships onboarded.
- 2,000+ active listings with at least 40% verified signals coverage.
- VerifiedTransactionRate established and trending upward.
- CAC payback trajectory validated for paid channels.

## Operating Model
- War-room cadence:
  - Daily ops standup (30 min)
  - Weekly growth review
  - Bi-weekly product-risk review
- Single accountable owner per workstream:
  - Growth, B2B, Trust Ops, Support, Data

## 90-Day Timeline

## Days 1-15: Pre-Launch Readiness
- Finalize launch messaging: trust, transparency, verified value.
- Activate dealer wave 1 (8-10 anchors).
- Configure analytics dashboards and alert thresholds.
- Run soft-launch QA with internal and invited users.

Deliverables:
- Partner launch kits.
- Support scripts and escalation matrix.
- City-level inventory quality baseline.

## Days 16-45: Launch and Stabilize
- Launch paid acquisition in both cities with city-specific creatives.
- Run C2C "Free VIN Check Week" campaign.
- Push dealer co-marketing (featured inventory slots).
- Operate trust review queue with strict SLA.

Expected focus:
- Supply liquidity and trust signal adoption.
- Response-time discipline for dealership leads.

## Days 46-75: Optimize Conversion
- Segment campaigns by verified vs non-verified inventory.
- Introduce comparison tool and price insight nudges.
- Optimize search ranking to reward trust completeness.
- Start retention programs for repeat buyers and top dealers.

Expected focus:
- Increase lead-to-visit and lead-to-sale performance.
- Reduce disputes and low-intent inquiries.

## Days 76-90: Scale Decision Gate
- Evaluate PMF and unit economics by segment and city.
- Prepare scale-up recommendation:
  - expand cities, expand channels, expand partner depth.
- Freeze lessons learned and update product roadmap.

## GTM Channel Plan
- Performance ads:
  - Search, social retargeting, lookalike audiences from verified leads.
- Content and trust education:
  - "How to buy safely" short videos and explainers.
- Dealer co-marketing:
  - Verified dealer spotlights and inventory drops.
- Referral mechanics:
  - Seller and buyer referral incentives with anti-abuse controls.

## Offer and Packaging
- C2C:
  - Free listing baseline.
  - Paid upgrades: verified fast-track, premium visibility.
- B2B:
  - Starter, Growth, Pro tiers.
  - Add-ons: featured placement, lead routing automation.

## KPI Cadence
- Daily:
  - Active listings, verification funnel, lead volume, SLA breaches.
- Weekly:
  - City-wise conversion, CAC by channel, dealer performance.
- Monthly:
  - VerifiedTransactionRate trend, B2B MRR, retention and disputes.

## Risk Register and Mitigation

## 1) Government/Partner Integration Delays
- Trigger: API downtime or onboarding delays.
- Mitigation:
  - Connector fallback and manual verification mode.
  - Clear user freshness labels and status messaging.
- Owner: Integrations lead.

## 2) Low Trust Signal Adoption by Sellers
- Trigger: sellers skip verification steps.
- Mitigation:
  - Ranking boost and visual badges for verified listings.
  - Limited visibility for incomplete trust profiles.
- Owner: Product growth lead.

## 3) Dealer Response SLA Failures
- Trigger: median response time degrades beyond target.
- Mitigation:
  - SLA alerts, manager escalation, temporary ranking penalty.
- Owner: B2B operations.

## 4) Fraud/Dispute Spike
- Trigger: dispute rate rises above threshold.
- Mitigation:
  - Add manual review sampling, stricter publish checks.
  - Fast-track dispute response and communications.
- Owner: Trust ops.

## 5) CAC Inflation
- Trigger: paid channel costs rise with weak conversion.
- Mitigation:
  - Shift budget to high-intent channels and dealer co-marketing.
  - Improve landing page trust proof and lead forms.
- Owner: Growth lead.

## 6) Data Quality Degradation
- Trigger: partner payload inconsistencies or stale updates.
- Mitigation:
  - Schema validation gates, freshness alarms, partner incident protocol.
- Owner: Data platform.

## Incident Severity Matrix
- Sev1: trust/security breach or critical partner outage.
- Sev2: major conversion or lead routing outage.
- Sev3: partial feature degradation with workaround.

Response standards:
- Sev1: acknowledge <= 15 min, public status updates every 30 min.
- Sev2: acknowledge <= 30 min, updates hourly.
- Sev3: acknowledge <= 2 hours, daily updates until closure.

## Pilot Exit Gate (Scale/No-Scale)
Scale decision requires:
- VerifiedTransactionRate positive momentum for 6+ weeks.
- Dealer retention >= 85%.
- Dispute rate within threshold and no unresolved critical incidents.
- Channel mix with at least one profitable acquisition path.

No-scale conditions:
- Persistent trust regression, unresolved legal blockers, or non-viable economics.
